Pro Outlaw Rules

General Rules

  1. NQS Super Stock and NQS Pro V-Twin run together. NQS Super Stock runs at 1050 lbs with 13" hitch and NQS Pro V-twins run at 1000 lbs. and a 12.5" hitch height.
  2. These rules can be changed in order to promote fairness of the type's tractors at anytime during the season.
