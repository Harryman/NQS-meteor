Pro V-twin Rules

Home | General Rules

  1. 1050 lbs. max.
  2. Maximum engine size is 45.5 cubic inches
  3. Engine shielding rules: 1/8" steel or 3/16 aluminum on external side of cylinder extending from head gasket to frame. Shield must be attached to frame at the bottom and to the flywheel shield at the top to make rigid.
  4. Wheelbase 56 inches maximum.
  5. Overall length 96 inches maximum.
  6. Engines: Must be a commercially available lawn and/or garden tractor engine. Only configuration accepted is V-twin type.
  7. Cylinder Head: Must be stock appearing cast head. No valve restrictions
  8. Carburetor: Limited to one (1) carburetor with one (1) venturi with one (1) throttle butterfly. Maximum venturi size will be 1.200". No air entering after the 1.200" restriction.
  9. Steel flywheel mandatory, along with the general flywheel shielding rules of 1/8" steel or 3/16" aluminum 360 degrees.
  10. Fuel: Methanol or Gas. See Rule 5
